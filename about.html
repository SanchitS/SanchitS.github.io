How I work

After reading and getting inspired by the book "Elements of User Experience" by Jesse James Garrett, I often apply the 5 fundamentals of UX defined in the book in the projects.

you will find these fundamentals properly defined and elaborated in almost every project in the WORK section.

For the Strategy, I love to collaborate with the clients, customers, people and end users for the Objectives, Business Needs, User needs and user research by brainstorming, talking to and learning from the users and working closely with my team.

Next step is for Scope where I look for all type of requirements in the project like technical, content and etc. and prioritizing the work on the basis of MoSCoW(Must, Should, Could, Would).

After getting it done I go for the Structure where I build the concrete, mapping workflows, conceptual models, information architecture, language and diagrams type of stuff.

When structure gets ready and gets verified from the business end me and my team run the clock for preparing the Skeleton by making the blueprints, Interface designs, Navigation, low+high fidelity wireframes.

Now the Structure is ready, the final step is to prepare the interfaces, layouts, pixel perfect design components, in short Surface of the product.
