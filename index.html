ABOUT

I am Sanchit, a passionate, creative individual capable to create graphic solutions for any communicational issue. With experience in web, graphics, UI/UX, visual design, I maintain a healthy balance between functionality and visual impact in all my work. I believe every piece of design can be a work of art.

I love my work and enjoy each new project as I get it. Feel free to have a look at my portfolio and don't hesitate to contact me if you think I can be of service to you.
